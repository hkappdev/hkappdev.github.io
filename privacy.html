Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 31, 2025

Overview

WinZ ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information Collection

WinZ does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The App operates entirely locally on your device and does not:

  • Collect personal data
  • Track your usage
  • Send data to external servers
  • Use analytics or telemetry
  • Access your files or documents
  • Monitor your browsing history

Permissions

The App requires the following Windows permissions to function:

  • Window Management: To detect and switch between open windows
  • Keyboard Hooks: To detect the Alt+Backtick keyboard shortcut
  • Startup Task: To run automatically when Windows starts (optional)

These permissions are used solely for the App's window-switching functionality.

Data Storage

The App stores minimal configuration data locally on your device, such as:

  • User preferences (if any)
  • Application settings

This data never leaves your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

Third-Party Services

WinZ does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. No information is collected from any users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact: support@example.com

Microsoft Store

When you download WinZ from the Microsoft Store, Microsoft may collect information according to their own privacy policy. Please review the Microsoft Privacy Statement for more information.
